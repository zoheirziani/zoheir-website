Educational Background

I started my academic journey at Université Mohammed Premier in Oujda, where I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Sciences de la Matière Physique (SMP). This rigorous program provided me with a strong foundation in fundamental physics, mathematics, and scientific methodologies. During my studies, I developed a deep understanding of classical mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and quantum physics, along with practical laboratory skills that allowed me to apply theoretical concepts to real-world experiments.

Driven by a passion for optics and materials science, I continued my studies with a Master’s degree in Optics and Materials. This specialized program allowed me to explore advanced topics such as wave optics, optical materials, thin films, photonic devices, and the interaction between light and matter. Through various research projects and hands-on laboratory work, I gained experience in experimental techniques, characterization methods, and numerical modeling, all of which are essential for understanding and designing optical and material-based technologies.
